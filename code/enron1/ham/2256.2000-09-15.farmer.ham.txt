Subject: enrononline launch monday 18 th.
We are pleased to announce that as of monday, sept. 18, the new version of
Enrononline will be launched to all our customers.
When our customers log in on this day, they will be given the option to
Upgrade to the new version. They can choose to immediately upgrade, or to
Upgrade at a later time.
If the customers choose to upgrade, they will be taken through the same steps
As when you upgraded. If the customers choose not to upgrade, they will
Receive a version of enrononline which is the same as the" old" version, but
The colors will be blue, instead of black.
Customers who choose not to immediately upgrade can can do so at a later time
By pressing the" upgrade to version 2" button in the top left corner of their
Screen.
Some customers may have browser or firewall restrictions which could cause
Difficulties in upgrading to the new version of enrononline. We have
Enrononline help desk and it troubleshooters standing by to help anyone in
This situation. For assistance, please direct calls to:

Www. Enrononline. Com to get to the new version.
